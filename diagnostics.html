Clear Diagnostic Boundaries

Quick check first. Deeper testing only after a quote.

The $25 mobile check is for quick issues that can be reasonably assessed in about 20 minutes. If the vehicle needs real diagnostic labor, we explain the next step and quote it before continuing.

How It Works

We do not guess and throw parts.

Listen

We collect the symptoms, warning lights, mileage, recent repairs, timeline, and whether the vehicle starts or drives.

Check Simple Causes

We look for the obvious cause first. If the answer is clear without deeper testing, the mobile check starts at $25.

Quote Deep Work

If the issue requires teardown, disassembly, tracing, pressure testing, leak testing, or deeper troubleshooting, we quote it first.

Where $25 Ends

Real diagnosis is skilled labor.

The $25 check keeps the first step fair for simple issues. It does not cover unlimited troubleshooting. That boundary protects the schedule and keeps pricing honest.
